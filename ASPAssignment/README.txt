This application can record customers' personal information and according their own account to record their order. 
Customer has first name, last name and address in their personal information.
Order has information about food type , amount of the food and price of the food.
One order only belong to one customer but a customer can have many order record.
Customer table uses orderId to connect order table to check what food the customer order. 
In order page, concatenate tow column in Customer table and show the result in view order.
